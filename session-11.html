💬 Idioms

Fashion Idioms

Common English idioms about clothing and fashion. Each includes meaning, example, and Arabic hint.

👔 Dressed to the nines

Meaning: Wearing very fancy or elegant clothes

Example: "She was dressed to the nines for the gala."

Arabic: لابس/ة أحلى لبس

🧤 Fit like a glove

Meaning: Fit perfectly

Example: "The new jacket fits like a glove."

Arabic: مقاسه مظبوط

❤ Wear your heart on your sleeve

Meaning: Show your feelings openly

Example: "He always wears his heart on his sleeve."

Arabic: يظهر مشاعره

💪 Roll up your sleeves

Meaning: Get ready to work hard

Example: "It's time to roll up your sleeves and study."

Arabic: شمّر عن ساعديه

🎩 Have something up your sleeve

Meaning: Have a secret plan

Example: "I think she has something up her sleeve for the party."

Arabic: عنده خطة سرية

👟 Walk a mile in someone's shoes

Meaning: Understand someone's experience

Example: "Before you judge, walk a mile in their shoes."

Arabic: حط نفسك مكانه

👞 If the shoe fits

Meaning: If something is true about you, accept it

Example: "He said I'm always late. Well, if the shoe fits!"

Arabic: لو الكلام عليك اقبله

📖 Reading

Clothes for Different Situations

Choosing the right clothes for each situation is important. What you wear can show respect, keep you comfortable, and even make you feel more confident.

For a summer picnic, you should wear something light and comfortable. A t-shirt and pants or shorts are perfect. Don't forget to bring a hat and sunglasses to protect yourself from the sun. Sneakers or sandals are good choices for your feet.

If you are going on a business trip, you need to look professional. A suit with a shirt and tie is the best choice for men. Women can wear a dress or formal pants with a blouse. Make sure your shoes are formal — no sneakers or flip-flops!

At the office, it's important to look neat and professional. Formal shoes are the best option. You can wear dress pants or a skirt with a nice shirt or blouse. Avoid wearing casual clothes like t-shirts and jeans unless your office allows it.

For exercise, comfort is the most important thing. Wear a t-shirt and shorts or track pants. Good sneakers will protect your feet while you run or walk. Don't wear heavy clothes — they will make it hard to move.

When it's cold outside, you need warm clothes. A heavy sweater, a winter coat, and a hat will keep you warm. Don't forget your mittens and a scarf! Boots are better than sandals in the winter.

Remember, the right outfit depends on the occasion. Whether it's a party, work, or a day at the park, choosing the right clothes will help you feel great!

🎭 Roleplay

Roleplay Scenarios

🛍 Scenario 1: Shopping

  • You are in a clothing store
  • Ask for help finding a formal outfit for a job interview
  • Your partner plays the shop assistant

You: Hi, I have a job interview next week. Can you help me find something formal?

Assistant: Of course! What kind of job is it?

You: It's an office job. I think I need a suit and a nice shirt.

Assistant: How about this navy suit with a white shirt and a tie?

You: That looks great! Do you have formal shoes too?

Assistant: Yes, these black leather shoes would be perfect. They fit like a glove!

🎉 Scenario 2: Fashion Advice

  • Your friend is going to a party and asks what to wear
  • Give them suggestions
  • Use vocabulary from the session

Friend: I'm going to a birthday party tonight. What should I wear?

You: Is it a formal or casual party?

Friend: It's at a nice restaurant, so I think it's a bit formal.

You: Then you should wear a dress or a blouse with a skirt. You'll look dressed to the nines!

Friend: What about shoes?

You: Don't wear sneakers! Go with some elegant shoes. And add some accessories like a watch or a necklace.

🌦 Scenario 3: Weather Discussion

  • Talk about what clothes you wear in different seasons
  • Your partner describes their seasonal outfits too
  • Use winter, summer, and casual vocabulary

You: What do you usually wear in summer?

Friend: I love wearing t-shirts, shorts, and sandals. What about you?

You: Same! But in winter, I always wear a warm coat, a scarf, and boots.

Friend: Me too. Don't forget gloves and a hat!

You: Yes! And I need comfortable clothes for studying. Usually jeans and a sweater.

Friend: That sounds very casual and stylish at the same time!

Quick Cheat Sheet

📚 Grammar Rules

Regular plurals: -s, -es, -ies, -ves
Irregular: man→men, woman→women, child→children
Uncountable (no plural): information, water, sugar
Always plural: pants, jeans, glasses

💬 Useful Expressions

I usually wear...
This outfit is perfect for...
You look great in that!
For this occasion, I would choose...

🗣 Speaking Rescue Phrases

"What I like to wear is..."
"For this occasion, I would choose..."
"My favorite clothing item is..."

⚠ Common Mistakes

✗ "informations" → ✓ "information"
✗ "My jeans is blue" → ✓ "My jeans are blue"
✗ "a pants" → ✓ "a pair of pants"

📋 OSASCOMP Rule

When using multiple adjectives, follow this order:

LetterCategoryExamples
OOpinionbeautiful, ugly, nice, lovely
SSizebig, small, long, short, tiny
AAge/Shapenew, old, round, square
SShaperound, square, rectangular
CColorred, blue, green, black
OOriginItalian, Egyptian, French
MMaterialcotton, leather, silk, wool
PPurposerunning (shoes), wedding (dress)
Example: “a beautiful (O) long (S) red (C) Italian (O) silk (M) evening (P) dress”

📖 Reading

Shopping for Clothes

Sarah and Emma went shopping at the mall. Sarah wanted to buy a new dress for a party. She found a beautiful long red silk dress. “This is gorgeous!” she said.

Emma was looking for something more casual. She picked up a nice short-sleeved cotton t-shirt with a floral pattern. “I love the colors!” she said.

Then they went to the shoe section. Sarah tried on some elegant black leather high heels. Emma chose comfortable white cotton sneakers. “These are perfect for walking!” Emma said.

Before leaving, they stopped at the accessories section. Sarah bought a small round gold necklace, and Emma found a big striped wool scarf. They both agreed: shopping is more fun with a friend!

🎭 Roleplay

Roleplay Scenarios

🛍 Scenario 1: At the Clothes Shop

  • Ask about sizes, colors, and materials
  • Describe what you want using adjective order
  • Give compliments to your friend

You: I'm looking for a nice long-sleeved cotton shirt. Do you have one in blue?

Shop: Yes! We have a beautiful new blue cotton one. What size?

You: Medium, please. And my friend needs a pair of elegant black leather shoes.

Shop: These Italian leather shoes just arrived!

Friend: Wow, those are gorgeous! They'll knock everyone's socks off!

👗 Scenario 2: Giving Compliments

  • Compliment your friend's outfit
  • Use adjective order correctly
  • Use fashion idioms

You: Wow! That's a gorgeous short red silk dress! You look amazing!

Friend: Thank you! I bought it at the new mall. It was expensive but it fits like a glove!

You: You're dressed to the nines! And those elegant small gold earrings are perfect!

Friend: You look great too! I love your big striped wool scarf!
